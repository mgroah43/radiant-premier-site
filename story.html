Why We Started

Homeowners Deserved Better Than the Usual Experience

"Do it right, or don't do it at all."

We spent years working inside larger window companies and saw the same problems over and over again. Once the sale was made, the homeowner often became an afterthought. Communication got weak. Install quality varied. And too many people ended up paying premium prices for an experience that didn't feel premium at all.

The problem wasn't the products. Window and door technology has never been better. The real problem was the gap between the manufacturer and the homeowner, and the lack of accountability in the middle.

Radiant Premier was built to close that gap. We took the experience we gained in the industry and built a company around the things that matter most: quality products, clean installation, honest communication, and long-term value for the homeowner.

Our Philosophy

Better Products, Better Installation, Better Experience

We are not trying to be the biggest company in the market. We are focused on being the company homeowners trust when they want the job done carefully and correctly. That means being selective about the products we stand behind, the people we bring onto a jobsite, and the way we communicate from start to finish.

Premium Should Mean More Than a Sales Pitch

A lot of companies use the word "premium" because it sounds good. For us, it means performance, durability, and attention to detail. It means recommending products that make sense for your home and standing behind the work after installation is complete.

Installation Matters Just as Much as the Product

Even a great window or door can underperform if it's installed poorly. That is why we focus so heavily on the details that many homeowners never see but absolutely benefit from: proper prep, correct sealing, clean finish work, and installation methods that protect long-term performance.

We Want the Experience to Feel Different

We believe homeowners should feel informed, respected, and confident throughout the process. No pressure. No shortcuts. No confusion about what comes next. Just a clear process, strong communication, and work that reflects the investment you're making in your home.

What We Stand On

Built on Discipline, Accountability, and Pride in the Work

Being veteran and minority owned is part of who we are, but more importantly, it shapes how we operate. We believe in showing up prepared, communicating clearly, and doing the work the right way even when no one is watching.

Those values aren't just part of our story. They are part of every estimate, every installation, and every homeowner relationship we build.

  • Accountability Without Excuses

    If we make a commitment, we stand behind it. If something needs attention, we address it directly.

  • Discipline in the Details

    From product selection to installation finish work, we care about the details that separate average jobs from lasting ones.

  • Service Before Pressure

    We are here to guide homeowners, not push them. We want the right solution, not just the fastest close.
