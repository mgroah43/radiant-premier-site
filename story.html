How It Started

We Saw What Was Broken. We Chose to Fix It.

Every industry has its shortcuts. In window and door replacement, those shortcuts come at the homeowner’s expense — budget products presented as premium, installations handed to whoever is available, warranties that disappear the moment something goes wrong.

After more than 15 combined years inside this industry, we saw the same story play out hundreds of times. Homeowners who trusted contractors, paid good money, and ended up with products that underperformed and craftsmanship that didn’t last.

Radiant Premier was founded on a single conviction: homeowners in the Dallas-Fort Worth area deserve better. Not marginally better — genuinely, measurably better. Better products. Better installation. A better experience from the first call to the final walkthrough.

So we built the company we wished existed. And we’ve run it the same way from day one.

What We Believe

Better Products, Better Installation, Better Experience

Products That Actually Perform

We made a decision early on: we would only carry products we’d install in our own homes. That means Alside windows with triple-pane glass and performance specs that most contractors don’t even quote. It means ProVia doors with insulation values and build quality that justify the investment. It means saying no to budget lines, no to mid-tier options, and no to anything we can’t fully stand behind.

A window that fails in year four isn’t a bargain at any price. We don’t sell bargains. We sell performance.

Installation Done to Spec — Every Time

The best window in the world installed incorrectly will underperform a mediocre window installed right. Installation matters as much as the product itself — and most contractors treat it as an afterthought.

We don’t use subcontractors. Ever. Every installation is performed by our own factory-certified team, trained to manufacturer specification. That’s not a marketing line — it’s how we protect your warranty and ensure the work we do is work we’re proud to put our name on.

An Experience Worth Recommending

Most homeowners dread dealing with contractors. We set out to change that. From the first call to the final cleanup, we treat your home and your time with respect. We communicate clearly, show up when we say we will, and don’t leave until the job is done right and the space is clean.

We believe that if we do this work the way it should be done, you’ll tell your neighbors. That’s how we grow — not through pressure tactics or slick marketing, but through homeowners who trust us enough to refer us.
