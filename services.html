Built-In Technology

Every Mezzo is Engineered, Not Just Assembled

EdgeForce™ Frame

Low-profile narrowline frame expands the glass area while internal chambers increase structural integrity, rigidity, and energy efficiency.

CoreFX™ Composite Reinforcement

Reduces energy transfer through the frame while strengthening meeting rails and allowing secure mounting of hardware.

Cavity Foam Insulation

Silver-gray graphite EPS foam lines select frame chambers, reflecting radiant heat and lowering thermal conductivity beyond standard vinyl.

Gatekeeper™ Sill Interlock

Interlocking sash-to-sill technology creates a unified wall of strength, helping prevent bowing in high-wind conditions.

Defense-Tek™ Locking

End-of-throw cam shift locking action delivers increased security and protection — engineered for both performance and peace of mind.

HP3™ Sill Dam System

Telescoping sill dam delivers protection from air and water infiltration, increases structural stability, and maintains a clean exterior appearance.
